Job Description

A web designer is a professional who is responsible for creating the visual design and layout of websites.A web designer typically works closely with clients or stakeholders to understand their needs and goals for the website, and then uses their expertise to create a design that meets those requirements.
In addition to designing the overall look and feel of a website, web designers may also be responsible for creating or selecting graphics, optimizing images for web use, and selecting appropriate fonts and layouts. They may also work with other professionals, such as developers and content creators, to ensure that the website is both visually appealing and user-friendly. The role of a web designer is crucial in the development of any website, as they are responsible for creating the first impression that visitors have of the site.
